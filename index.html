About

Our College Environmental

We believe that TECH is a place where “Change” happens. Our students are challenged and motivated to change their perspectives by our faculties.

Our faculties constantly change their pedagogies and instructional approaches to match industry requirements and student needs.t is virtuous cycle of “change” that happens only in the beautiful and serene college of TECH.

VISION- To be a leading educational organization imparting holistic education to help students become responsible world citizens who are sensitive to the needs of the society.

  • I want students at TECH to feel happy about learning, about going to class.
  • Learning should be relevant to their life, and they must feel that their experience at TECH was a worthwhile one.
  • It is leading one of the fastest growing and most promising educational institution of the country.

MISSION-To develop a community of students and academicians who are a part of world class education system which is developed in a manner which supports the intellectual, professional and moral growth of the students leading to advancement of human knowledge through enterprising research.

CHECK OUR ACADEMICS

Medical

A medical course trains students to become healthcare professionals, covering subjects like anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, and clinical practice.

Engineering

An engineering course teaches problem-solving and technical skills in fields like mechanical, civil, electrical, and computer engineering. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications through labs and projects.

Management

A management course equips students with leadership, strategic planning, and organizational skills. It covers subjects like finance, marketing, human resources, and operations management.

Agriculture

An agriculture course focuses on farming techniques, soil science, crop production, and livestock management.It includes practical training in modern agricultural technologies and sustainability practices.

Doctoral Studies

Doctoral studies focus on advanced research, critical analysis, and expertise in a specific field. Students conduct original research, write a dissertation, and contribute new knowledge to their discipline.

Pharmacy

A pharmacy course covers drug formulation, medical chemistry, pharmacology, and patient care. It includes theoretical learning, laboratory work, and clinical training.
